Lucid Dreams is the very impressive follow-up to Arturo Mayorga’s 2009 debut, Cascades. As he did on his first album, Mayorga has included an interesting variety of musical styles and moods that demonstrate his versatility as a composer and pianist/keyboardist. One of the eleven tracks is solo piano, and the others include guitar, flute, percussion and drums, wordless vocals, oboe, and/or strings. Some of the pieces indicate Yanni’s influence...
